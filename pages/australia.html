PTE Academic in Australia

Your pathway to Australian universities and professional success

University Admissions

PTE is accepted by all major Australian universities as proof of English proficiency. Most universities require a minimum PTE score of 50-61 for undergraduate programs.

Visa Requirements

Australian immigration accepts PTE for Student Visa applications. PTE scores are valid for 3 years and are recognized by the Department of Home Affairs.

Widely Accepted

PTE Academic is accepted by over 3,000+ institutions worldwide, including every major Australian university and professional bodies.

Quick Results

Get your PTE results within 5 business days. Fast turnaround helps you apply to universities without delays and secure your admission quickly.

Student Visa Information

Everything you need to know about Australian student visas

Visa Subclass 500

The primary visa subclass for international students. Allows you to study full-time and work part-time during semester (20 hours/week) and full-time during breaks.

Requirements

  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from an Australian institution
  • Proof of English proficiency (PTE, IELTS, TOEFL)
  • Evidence of financial capacity (minimum AUD $20,000-$25,000/year)
  • Health insurance (OSHC - Overseas Student Health Cover)
  • Health examination (if required)

Processing Time

Australian student visas are typically processed within 10-21 days for eligible applicants. Apply 3-4 months before your intended start date for timely approval.

Visa Validity

Student visas are valid for the duration of your course plus 6 months (or graduation date + 6 months, whichever is earlier). Plan your post-graduation work timeline accordingly.
