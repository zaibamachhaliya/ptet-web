What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

Note: Cookies do not contain viruses or malware, and they cannot access other information on your device. They are simply used to improve your browsing experience and help us understand how our site is used.

Cookies can be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services used on that site ("third-party cookies"). They may be stored temporarily for a session or persistently on your device for a set period.

How We Use Cookies

PTE HUB uses cookies for several important purposes:

  • Site functionality — To keep you logged in and maintain your session as you navigate between pages.
  • Preferences — To remember your settings such as light or dark theme and language preferences.
  • Performance & analytics — To understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve content and usability.
  • Security — To protect against fraudulent activity and ensure secure access to your account.

We do not use cookies to serve targeted advertising or sell your data to advertisers.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or filling in forms. Without these cookies, services you have asked for cannot be provided.

Preference Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred theme) and provide enhanced, more personalised features. The information these cookies collect may be anonymised and they cannot track your browsing activity on other websites.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with PTE HUB by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve page layouts, content, and overall user experience. We do not use this data to identify individual users.

Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, we use services provided by trusted third parties that may also set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Fonts — Used to load typography assets. Google may set cookies as part of this service.
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs) — Used to serve icon assets via a CDN.
  • Vercel (hosting) — Our hosting provider may set performance and routing cookies.

These third-party services have their own privacy and cookie policies. We recommend reviewing them directly on their respective websites. PTE HUB does not control these third-party cookies and is not responsible for how they are used.

No advertising cookies: We do not integrate with any advertising networks (such as Google Ads or Meta Pixel). No cookies are used on this site for tracking you across other websites for advertising purposes.

Cookie Duration

Cookies we use fall into two duration categories:

  • Session cookies — These exist only while your browser is open. They are deleted automatically when you close your browser. We use these for login sessions and temporary security tokens.
  • Persistent cookies — These remain on your device for a set period (shown in the table above) or until you delete them manually. We use these for theme preferences and analytics.

Managing Your Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Browser settings — Most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies through their settings. See the section below for browser-specific instructions.
  • Third-party opt-outs — You can opt out of analytics tracking directly through third-party providers where applicable.
  • Delete existing cookies — You can clear all cookies stored by PTE HUB at any time through your browser's history or privacy settings.

Please note that blocking essential cookies will affect the functionality of the site. You may not be able to log in or use certain features if essential cookies are disabled.

Browser-Specific Settings

To manage cookies in your browser, follow the instructions for your browser below:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Opera: Settings → Advanced → Privacy & security → Site Settings → Cookies

For other browsers, please refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions on managing cookies.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please be aware of the following potential impacts on your experience:

  • You may not be able to log in or stay logged in to your account.
  • Your theme preference (light/dark mode) will not be remembered between visits.
  • Some pages or features may not load or function correctly.
  • You may be prompted to re-enter information more frequently.

Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the core features of PTE HUB. It only means we receive less information about how the site is being used, which may slow our ability to improve the experience over time.
