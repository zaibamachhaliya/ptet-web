What is PTE Academic?

PTE Academic assesses real-life English communication skills. It uses AI to deliver fair, unbiased results – often within 48 hours. No human bias, no unexpected surprises.

Test Format

The exam takes ~2 hours and includes 20 different task types.

Speaking & Writing

Read aloud, repeat sentences, describe image, summarize text – 54–67 min.

Reading

Multiple choice, reorder paragraphs, fill in the blanks – 29–30 min.

Listening

Summarize spoken text, highlight incorrect words, write from dictation – 30–43 min.

Scoring System

Overall score range: 10 – 90

PTE uses automated scoring based on thousands of real test-takers. Each skill (grammar, oral fluency, pronunciation, spelling, vocabulary, written discourse) is scored individually. Results are typically available within 48 hours.

Enabling Skills

Grammar, oral fluency, pronunciation, spelling, vocabulary.

Communicative Skills

Listening, Reading, Speaking, Writing – each scored 10–90.

Fast Reporting

Scores sent to unlimited institutions for free.
