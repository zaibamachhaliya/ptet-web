PTE Academic in New Zealand

Your pathway to New Zealand universities and professional success

University Admissions

PTE is accepted by all major New Zealand universities as proof of English proficiency. Most universities require a minimum PTE score of 50-58 for undergraduate programs.

Student Visa Requirements

New Zealand Immigration accepts PTE for Student Visa applications. PTE scores are valid for 3 years and recognized by Immigration New Zealand.

Widely Accepted

PTE is accepted by 3,000+ institutions worldwide, including all major New Zealand universities and professional bodies across New Zealand.

Quick Results

Get your PTE results within 5 business days. Fast turnaround helps you apply to universities without delays and secure admission quickly.

Student Visa Information

Everything you need to know about New Zealand student visas

Student Visa Basics

Required to study at approved New Zealand institutions as an international student. Allows you to study full-time and work part-time.

Key Requirements

  • Offer of place from an approved educational institution
  • Proof of English proficiency (PTE, IELTS, TOEFL)
  • Evidence of financial capacity (NZD $15,000-$20,000/year)
  • Medical examination (if required)
  • Police clearance (if applicable)

Processing Time

Student visa applications are typically processed within 2-4 weeks for most countries. Apply 2-3 months before your intended start date for timely approval.

Visa Validity

Student visas are valid for the duration of your course plus 6 months. After graduation, you may be eligible for a Post-Study Work Visa.
